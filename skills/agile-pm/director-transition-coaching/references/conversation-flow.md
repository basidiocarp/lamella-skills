# Director Transition Coaching Example

This example shows a senior PM preparing for Director interviews with a weak strategic narrative.

## Diagnostic Flow

1. Confirm the current stage:
   - preparing
   - interviewing
   - newly landed
   - recalibrating
2. Identify the main gap:
   - delegation and letting go of IC work
   - leading through other PMs
   - strategic narrative and context translation
   - managing up and cross-functional influence
3. Confirm the timing pressure:
   - immediate
   - next 3 to 6 months
   - longer runway

## Example Output

For a PM who is 3 to 6 months from interviews and weak on strategic narrative:

1. Build a weekly context cascade from company priority to team implication.
2. Reframe 1:1s to ask for Director-level decisions and trade-offs.
3. Prepare stories about creating clarity, developing others, and handling portfolio trade-offs.
4. Audit hero behavior for two weeks and hand off recurring rescue work.
5. Replace "I shipped" language with leverage, systems, and judgment language.

## Why This Example Matters

- It shows the skill as a short diagnostic conversation, not a generic pep talk.
- It produces practical next moves tied to the user's current stage.
- It keeps the focus on transition leverage rather than abstract career advice.
