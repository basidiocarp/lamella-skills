# Storyboard Template

Use this structure to draft a six-frame storyboard.

```markdown
## 6-Frame Storyboard: [Working Title]

**Frame 1: Main Character**
- Who they are
- What context they are in

**Frame 2: Problem Emerges**
- What frustrates or blocks them
- Why it matters

**Frame 3: Escalation**
- What turns the problem into an urgent issue

**Frame 4: Solution Appears**
- How they encounter the solution
- Why they try it

**Frame 5: Breakthrough**
- What changes in the first meaningful moment

**Frame 6: Life After**
- What is better now
- What measurable or observable change proves it

**Optional Visual Style**
- Default: minimal monochrome storyboard sketches
- Or list the specific visual direction the team wants
```
