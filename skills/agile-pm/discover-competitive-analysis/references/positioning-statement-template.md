# Positioning Statement Template

Use this when the competitive analysis needs a crisp Geoffrey Moore-style position.

```markdown
## Positioning Statement

### Value Proposition
- **For:** [target customer or persona]
- **That Need:** [underserved need]
- **Product:** [product or service name]
- **Category:** [product category]
- **Benefit:** [main customer outcome]

### Differentiation
- **Unlike:** [primary competitor or alternative]
- **Product:** [product or service name]
- **Provides:** [unique differentiation]
```

## Stress-Test Questions

- Would a real customer recognize themselves in the target description?
- Is the need specific enough to prove with research?
- Is the category one buyers actually use?
- Is the differentiation durable and believable?
- Would this positioning help reject or prioritize roadmap choices?
