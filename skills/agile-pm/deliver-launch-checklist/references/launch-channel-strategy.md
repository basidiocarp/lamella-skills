# Launch Channel Strategy Notes

Use these notes when the checklist needs more launch-strategy detail than a simple readiness pass.

## ORB Framing

Cover all three channel types:

- **Owned:** email, blog, product surfaces, community
- **Rented:** ads, sponsorships, marketplaces, paid distribution
- **Borrowed:** partners, creators, press, communities you do not own

## Phased Launch Model

- alpha or private preview
- beta or early access
- public launch or GA
- post-launch momentum and follow-up

## Strategy Checks

- choose channels that match the audience, not generic launch habits
- assign owners and timing for each channel
- plan follow-up content so momentum does not die after launch day
- include launch-day and post-launch actions separately

## Special Cases

- Product Hunt needs explicit prep, listing quality, and day-of engagement
- pricing changes should be treated as launches, not silent edits
- feature launches still need a post-launch measurement and follow-up plan
