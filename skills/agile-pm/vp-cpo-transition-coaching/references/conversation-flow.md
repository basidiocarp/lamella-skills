# VP CPO Transition Coaching Example

This example shows a Director evaluating a near-offer VP Product role.

## Diagnostic Flow

1. Confirm the stage:
   - preparing
   - evaluating or interviewing
   - newly landed
   - recalibrating
2. Narrow the current pressure:
   - scope and expectations
   - empowerment and decision rights
   - executive team dynamics
   - vague or contradictory strategy
3. Turn the concern into a diligence or repair plan.

## Example Output

For a near-offer candidate with executive-dynamics risk:

1. Ask what decisions Product will own independently in the first 90 days.
2. Ask which major product decisions were reversed in the last year and why.
3. Ask where Product and GTM alignment breaks down most often.
4. Cross-check CEO answers with CRO and CTO separately.
5. Walk away if strategy, authority, and success criteria stay inconsistent across leaders.

## Why This Example Matters

- It converts vague executive anxiety into testable questions.
- It keeps the coaching decision-ready instead of inspirational.
- It distinguishes executive operating reality from generic leadership advice.
