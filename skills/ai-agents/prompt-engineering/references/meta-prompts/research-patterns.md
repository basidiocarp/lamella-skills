<overview>
Prompt patterns for research work that feeds later planning or implementation.
</overview>

<core_shape>

```xml
<objective>
Research {topic} for {downstream_goal}.
</objective>

<scope>
- What must be answered
- What is explicitly out of scope
- What counts as a primary source
</scope>

<output>
- Summary
- Findings
- Recommendations
- Open questions
- Confidence notes
</output>
```

</core_shape>

<rules>

- Prefer primary sources and dated evidence when the topic changes over time.
- Separate verified findings from inference.
- Keep the output directly consumable by the next prompt in the chain.

</rules>
