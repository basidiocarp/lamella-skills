# ADR Templates

Choose the ADR template based on decision weight and team formality.

## Common Formats

- standard MADR for major architecture decisions
- lightweight ADR for faster team use
- Y-statement for obvious concise choices
- deprecation ADR when reversing earlier decisions
- RFC-style ADR for large proposals needing discussion

## Rule

Pick the smallest format that still captures context, decision, consequences,
and ownership.
